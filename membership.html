Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your membership anytime. If you're on a monthly plan, you can cancel with 30 days notice. Annual plans have a 60-day cancellation notice.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a complimentary trial pass for 1 day to explore our facilities and meet our staff. Contact us to schedule your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. Payments are processed securely on the 1st of each month.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, we offer membership freeze options for up to 3 months due to travel, injury, or other circumstances. Contact our membership team for details.

Do you have family or group discounts?

Yes! Family memberships (3+ members) get 20% discount. Corporate groups get special rates. Contact sales@fitzone.com for more information.

What's included in the Elite membership?

Elite membership includes 24/7 gym access, unlimited classes, 2 personal training sessions per month, nutrition counseling, spa access, and unlimited guest privileges.
